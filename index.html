company info

CodeNOW is a cloud-based software delivery platform founded in 2014, designed to accelerate cloud-native development by automating DevOps processes, including CI/CD pipelines, infrastructure management, and monitoring. Based in Montclair, USA, with legal entities in the Czech Republic, the company focuses on helping businesses reduce infrastructure costs and improve time-to-market. The platform supports multi-cloud environments (AWS, GCP, Azure)
